About Our Project

Our project, Reversible Data Hiding in Encrypted Images, introduces a novel approach for securely embedding data in encrypted images without compromising their confidentiality.

The method is based on Edge-Directed Prediction and Multi-MSB Self-Prediction, ensuring high embedding capacity and low distortion while allowing full image recovery.

This technique is ideal for applications where privacy and data integrity are critical, such as secure medical imaging, cloud storage, and confidential communications.
